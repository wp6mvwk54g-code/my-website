1. VISUAL IDENTITY PACKAGE

A visual identity package to represent your business or professional service to the world. Think: professional, credible, yours.

LOGO AND STYLE GUIDE

  • Wordmark (typography) and/or pictorial mark (icon)
  • Printable style guide included
  • Two open-source fonts included

A style guide provides useful guidance on employing your visual identity system (logo, colors, fonts) in various contexts as business needs grow and change.

PRINT MATTER DESIGN

  • Letterhead
  • Envelope
  • Business card

Print matter designs feature logo, physical address, phone number, website URL, and/or email address.

Deliverables are digital; client handles printing.

SINGLE-PAGE STATIC SITE

  • One page, five sections
  • HTML/CSS/JavaScript (no blog/CMS/server-side code)
  • Client provides all copy and photography

A single-page site acts as a digital "brochure" for your business or service.

Instructions are provided, but domain and hosting are client's responsibility.

PRICE: $3,500. 50% nonrefundable deposit ($1,750).

ESTIMATED TURNAROUND: Eight weeks from start of work.

3. HOW THE PROCESS WORKS

Within a single business day of contacting me, I will email you an onboarding form (to be completed before work starts) to assess client tastes and needs.

ONBOARDING AND DEPOSIT

  1. Initial contact and onboarding form
  2. Design brief and contract sent to client to sign
  3. Work begins on payment of (nonrefundable) deposit

Within three business days of finishing my onboarding form, I will send you the contract, design brief, and instructions for payment of (nonrefundable) deposit.

DEVELOPMENT AND FEEDBACK

  1. Logo design (three phases, two rounds of feedback)
  2. Design of print matter and style guide
  3. Design of website (copy/images provided by client)

I like to give clients one week per phase of design for consideration and feedback.

The entire process generally takes eight weeks (but may vary slightly).

DELIVERABLES

  1. Logo: PDF, SVG, PNG
  2. Print matter: PDF
  3. Website: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

All files will be delivered in .ZIP format via email or other agreed-upon (digital) delivery method.

I do not provide deliverables via physical media.

4. OPTIONAL MONTHLY RETAINER

For $500/month, I offer a monthly retainer package as an add-on for existing clients to help maintain their visual identity across various brand touchpoints.

SOCIAL MEDIA GRAPHICS

  • Up to four (4) social media graphics per month
  • Will adhere to existing style guide
  • Copy and photography provided by client

Each social media graphic will come with multiple versions that should work for all major platforms. Posting graphics is client's responsibility.

BROCHURES, PERIODICALS, ETC.

  • Up to four (4) pages of printed content per month
  • Will adhere to existing style guide
  • Copy and photography provided by client

One "page" of content is equivalent to one side of a single 8.5 x 11" sheet of standard printing paper. A brochure can be made by folding into two, three, etc.

WEBMASTER

  • Up to four (4) small modifications or one new page
  • Will adhere to existing style guide
  • Copy and photography provided by client

"Small modifications" include: a new section, content change, or updated contact info. A "new" page is made up of five sections of client-generated content.

PRICE: $500/month, prepaid.

TURNAROUND: Varies depending on service rendered.
